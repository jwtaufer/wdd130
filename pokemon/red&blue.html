Pokemon Red & Pokemon Blue were the first of many Pokemon games to be released in the west in 1998. They followed after their eastern counterparts "Pocket Monsters: Red," "Pocket Monsters: Green" and "Pocket Monsters: Blue," the last of which was the base for the international version we know here in the west.
